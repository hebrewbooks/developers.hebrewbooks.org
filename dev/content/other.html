Linking to Other Content

This page describes how to link to other content we offer

Types of Content

Besides book scans, we also offer several specialized types of content as follows:

  • Main site (hebrewbooks.org) and Beta Site (beta.hebrewbooks.org):
    • Text version of the Friedberg Rambam and commentaries (https://hebrewbooks.org/rambam)
    • Scans/Text of the Babylonian Talmud (Shas) and links to related commentaries (https://hebrewbooks.org/shas)
  • Beta site only (beta.hebrewbooks.org):
    • Scanned version of the Rambam (https://beta.hebrewbooks.org/rambam)
    • Scans of the Arba Turim / Tur and Shulchan Aruch (https://beta.hebrewbooks.org/tursa)
  • Manuscripts site (hebrewmanuscripts.org):
    • Scans of manuscripts

At this time, we only offer a way to link to the Talmud as described below.

How to Link to the Babylonian Talmud / Shas

The canonical way to construct the links is af follows:

  • https://hebrewbooks.org/shas.aspx?mesechta=XX&daf=YYY&format=pdf
  • https://beta.hebrewbooks.org/shas.aspx?mesechta=XX&daf=YYY&format=pdf

The parameters are as follows - all without leading zeroes:

  • XX - is two digit mesechta / tractate number from the table below
  • YYY - is a three digit page number. Page numbers may include a side:
    • YYY - is YYY, side a (do not include a in the parameter)
    • YYYb - is YYY, side b
  • Z is the format which can be one of the following values:
    • pdf - PDF images
    • text - text only

The links will lead to the page itself, we do not yet offer a way to link to the related commentaries or calendars.

Example links:

  • Yomah 2b - PDF: https://beta.hebrewbooks.org/shas.aspx?mesechta=6&daf=2b&format=pdf
  • Gitin 46a - TEXT: https://hebrewbooks.org/shas.aspx?mesechta=19&daf=46&format=text
